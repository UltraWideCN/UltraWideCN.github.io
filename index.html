关于我们

清青超广角影视（UltraWide Media Studio）成立于 2024 年 5 月，诞生于清华园的创新沃土，以 "超广角・致青年" 为精神内核，致力于打造 "以硬核科技为骨，以人文情怀为魂" 的新型影像内容。我们秉持 "用青年视角解构宏大叙事，以帧率精度传递时代温度" 的使命，光影足迹从清华学堂延伸至中拉减贫营。

自成立以来，我们已构建起覆盖 6 大领域、与 20 + 顶级机构的深度合作网络。作品相继登陆人民日报、公安部刑侦局、国家反诈中心、清华大学等平台，打造出《三国反诈》《回声》《清春》等破圈佳作，单期作品最高播放量突破 5000 万次。

展望未来，我们将以 "自强不息" 的精神继续深耕影像领域，让青年影像成为跨越山海、对话世界的文化纽带。
UltraWide Media Studio, founded in May 2024, was born from the innovative environment of Tsinghua University. With the core spirit of "UltraWide: To the Youth," we are dedicated to creating a new type of visual content that is "rooted in hard-core technology and soulful with humanistic care." Upholding the mission of "deconstructing grand narratives from a youthful perspective and conveying the pulse of the era with frame-rate precision," our visual footprint extends from Tsinghua classrooms to the China-Latin America Poverty Reduction Camp.

Since our establishment, we have built a deep collaborative network covering 6 major fields and partnering with over 20 top institutions. Our works have been featured on platforms such as People's Daily, Criminal Investigation Department of the Ministry of Public Security, National Anti-Fraud Center, and Tsinghua University, creating viral hits like "Three Kingdoms Anti-Fraud," "Echo," and "Qingchun," with the highest single episode view count exceeding 50 million.

Looking ahead, we will continue to cultivate the field of visual media with the spirit of "Self-Improvement," making youth visual content a cultural bridge that crosses mountains and seas and dialogues with the world.
